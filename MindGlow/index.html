John's Journey

John, a passionate software developer from San Francisco, faced unprecedented challenges in his life. From battling severe bouts of anxiety due to the pressures of the tech world to managing personal life upheavals, his journey was tumultuous.

However, with the help of dedicated therapists and a supportive community, John embarked on a path to recovery. He tapped into the strength he never knew he had and slowly rebuilt his life, one day at a time. Today, John actively participates in support groups, sharing his experiences, and inspiring others to seek help and hope.

About Us

Our Mission

At MindGlow, we believe in the power of community and understanding. Our mission is to provide comprehensive mental health support, resources, and a platform for shared experiences.

Our Journey

MindGlow started as a small support group in 2015 and has grown to become a beacon of hope for many. Our platform is driven by stories, expertise, and the relentless pursuit of making mental health a priority for all.
